Samp Recipe

An easy to folow recipe for samp from the best chef.

INGREDIENTS

  • samp
  • butter
  • vegetables
  • eggs
  • soya sauce
  • salt to season

    • STEPS

    1. Boil your samp
    2. let it cool down and put it in the freezer for 25 minutes
    3. add butter to your pan
    4. add your mixed veges and fry
    5. add the samp
    6. add salt to season and some soya sauce
