Beef Stew Recipe

An easy to folow recipe for beef stew from the best chef.

INGREDIENTS

  • beef
  • butter
  • vegetables
  • soup mixture
  • salt to season

    • STEPS

    1. Boil your beef
    2. add butter to your pan
    3. fry your beef
    4. add your mixed veges and fry
    5. add some soup mixture of your choice
    6. add salt to season
